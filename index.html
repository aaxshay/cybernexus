360 Degree 3D capture solution

Automated 360 Product photography solution

A multi-axis imaging system with six programmable axes automates 360-degree multi-row (angle) product photography (also known as 3D product photography) to create highly interactive e-commerce images. Create sequences that include both camera movement and turntable rotation to control axes for consistent, repeatable movement.


Multi-Axis is our system with the ability to control the camera position allowing you to create a more dynamic view of your product with multi-row rotation and motion-triggered video, in addition to a single-row 360 rotation and still images.

ZESTPORTAL.COM - Automatic 360 Video Booth

Remote controlled and Wirelessly operable Automatic 360 Video Booth Platform

Zest 360 degree photobooth technology revolves almost any hand held video camera device in a 360-degree view around a central fixed platform. The end result of which is a seamless and breathtaking bullet-time effect video.


Zest 360 Degree photobooth is capable of carrying ~350Kg load on a 1M wide round platform. Being wireless having operational backup for hours, makes it capable to serve at any event's place possible. More stable and motor operated remotely controlled system enables hassle free capture of the videos.


Zest 360 Degree photobooth is available to serve for events at ad agencies, brands, and private events, rentals of the 360 degree photobooths.
